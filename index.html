11 Mixtape

⚠️ 11 Mixtape is developed by a teenager with basic technical knowledge. This is an experimental, unstable, and early-stage project. Bugs, design mistakes, and incomplete features are expected. Use at your own risk.
11 Mixtape is an experimental operating system kernel focused on security, isolation, and long-term learning. It does not aim to replace mainstream systems, but to explore how strict security decisions shape usability.
The kernel is still at a conceptual stage. Core components are being designed, rethought, or not yet implemented. Progress is slow by design.
Future versions may support limited daily use, including a graphical environment, basic browsing, media playback, and heavy visual customization (ricing). Gaming support will remain extremely limited.
