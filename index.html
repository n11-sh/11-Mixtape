11 Mixtape

⚠️ 11 Mixtape is an experimental, early-stage project developed by an individual. Expect instability, missing features, and radical design decisions. Use at your own risk.
11 Mixtape is an experimental operating system kernel focused on security-first design, architectural clarity, and intentional limitations.
While future versions may support limited daily use such as browsing, media playback, and heavy visual customization (ricing), the system is not intended for gaming or performance-heavy workloads.
