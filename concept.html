Concept & Philosophy

This page describes ideas, intentions, and long-term goals. Nothing here is guaranteed, finished, or stable.

What is 11 Mixtape?

11 Mixtape is an experimental operating system kernel focused on security, restriction, and architectural clarity. It exists as a learning project, not as a replacement for existing operating systems.

Security Before Comfort

Unlike mainstream systems, 11 Mixtape treats inconvenience as acceptable. Reduced compatibility, blocked behavior, and limited freedom are deliberate design choices when they improve system integrity.

Limited Daily Use

While originally designed as a security-focused system, future versions may support limited daily use: a graphical environment, basic web browsing, media playback, and visual customization (ricing). Gaming support will remain minimal and experimental.

11-Frontier

11-Frontier is a theoretical security boundary that analyzes files before they enter the internal system. Suspicious files are blocked, isolated, or deleted automatically. Trust is never assumed.

11-Medkit

11-Medkit is a planned emergency system designed to respond to compromises, infections, or unexpected behavior. Its goal is containment and recovery, not perfect detection.
