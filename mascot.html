Kira, the Red Panda

Kira is the official mascot of 11 Mixtape. She represents curiosity, caution, and quiet intelligence. Like the system itself, she is calm, observant, and defensive rather than aggressive.
The red panda was chosen not only because the creator deeply loves them, but because they symbolize rarity, gentleness, and survival in hostile environments. A perfect metaphor for a hardened operating system.
Kira is not just branding. She reflects the emotional core of the project: a personal system built with care, obsession, and stubborn dedication.
