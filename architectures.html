Planned Architectures

x86_64

x86_64 is planned as the primary development architecture due to its wide documentation, mature tooling, and availability on standard hardware. It allows easier debugging and experimentation during early stages.

ARM64

ARM64 is planned to support modern low-power devices and future experimentation with mobile, embedded, or alternative hardware. Supporting ARM early helps design architecture-agnostic core systems.

Why These Architectures?

Both architectures represent the present and future of computing. Supporting them allows 11 Mixtape to remain relevant, flexible, and educational without spreading development too thin.
